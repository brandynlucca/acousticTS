# Calculate the acoustic wavenumber (\\k\\) for a given frequency and sound speed.

Calculates the acoustic wavenumber (\\k\\) for a given frequency and
sound speed in water. The wavenumber is defined as: \$\$k = \frac{2\pi
f}{c}\$\$ where \\f\\ is the frequency (Hz) and \\c\\ is the sound speed
(\\ms^{-1}\\). The wavenumber describes the spatial frequency of a sound
wave and is fundamental in acoustic calculations.

## Usage

``` r
wavenumber(frequency, sound_speed)
```

## Arguments

- frequency:

  Frequency (f, Hz)

- sound_speed:

  Sound speed (c, \\m~s^{-1}\\)

## Value

Calculates the acoustic wavenumber (k) based on the sound speed of
water.
