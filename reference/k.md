# Calculate the acoustic wavenumber (k) based on the sound speed of water.

Calculate the acoustic wavenumber (k) based on the sound speed of water.

## Usage

``` r
k(frequency, sound_speed)
```

## Arguments

- frequency:

  Frequency (f, Hz)

- sound_speed:

  Sound speed (c, m/s)

## Value

Calculates the acoustic wavenumber (k) based on the sound speed of
water.
